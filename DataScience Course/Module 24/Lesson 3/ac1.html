🌐 Useful Web Resources

Welcome to this list of websites curated by Mimo. These platforms are excellent for gathering information and expanding your digital horizons.

Expanding Your Knowledge

Explore Wikipedia, a vast resource for learning about almost any topic imaginable.

Find new reading materials and join a community of readers at Goodreads to discuss books and ideas.

Discovering Space

Stay updated with the latest in space exploration, rocket launches, and science at NASA.

View incredible deep-space photography and astronomical discoveries at the James Webb Space Telescope portal.

Understanding Our Planet

Use NASA Eyes on the Earth to visualize satellites and environmental data in an interactive 3D environment.

Visit National Geographic to learn about wildlife, culture, and science around the globe.
