✨ The Art of Text Styling

Welcome to this guide created by Mimo. We are going to look at how different HTML tags allow us to give meaning and emphasis to our text.

Bold and Strong

Using the b tag makes text look bold for visual effect.

Using the strong tag tells browsers that the text is of high importance.

Italic and Emphasis

The i tag is typically used to set text apart, like titles or technical terms.

The em tag is used to add verbal stress or emphasis to a specific word.

Highlights and Subtle Text

You can use the mark tag to highlight information that needs attention.

The small tag is perfect for adding fine print or side notes that are less critical.

Strikethrough Text

The del tag is used to indicate text that has been removed or is no longer accurate.

It helps in showing changes or corrections clearly within your content.

Superscript and Subscript

The sup tag raises text slightly, which is useful for mathematical powers like x2.

The sub tag lowers text, commonly seen in chemical formulas like CO2.
