💡 Quotes That Motivate

Welcome to this collection curated by Mimo. We explore how short, powerful sentences can change our perspective on learning and life.

The Power of Ideas

Albert Einstein famously shared: Imagination is more important than knowledge.

The measure of intelligence is the ability to change.

The Value of Education

Malala Yousafzai taught us: One child, one teacher, one book, one pen can change the world.

I raise up my voice — not so that I can shout, but so that those without a voice can be heard.

Persistence

Nelson Mandela believed: Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.

It always seems impossible until it is done.

Discovery

Marie Curie once noted: Nothing in life is to be feared, it is only to be understood.

I was taught that the way of progress was neither swift nor easy.
